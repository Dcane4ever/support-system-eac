Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request for my academic documents?

Visit the Registrar's Office or submit a request through this portal. Processing time is typically 3-5 working days. Make sure to include your student ID and valid ID when making requests.

What are the payment methods available?

EAC accepts payments through bank transfer, online banking, credit/debit cards, and over-the-counter payments at our cashier's office. For specific payment instructions, please contact our Finance Department.

How do I reset my student portal password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the student portal login page or contact our IT Support desk for immediate assistance.

What are the enrollment procedures?

Enrollment procedures include assessment of previous semester grades, payment of fees, and subject registration. Visit our Registrar's Office or check the student portal for the complete enrollment guide.
